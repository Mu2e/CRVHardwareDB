Entries must be done in stages
1) Enter Purchase orders
2) Enter Recieved batches
3) Enter the SiPM Id number
4) Enter the Vendor SiPM values for
    a) Bias Voltage
    b) Dark Count
    c) Gain
    ** Note this is done for ~10% of SiPMs
5) The locally measured values for
    a) Bias Voltage
    b) Dark Count
    c) Gain
